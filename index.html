about

Athul is an artist and a photographer who resides in Charlotte, North Carolina. He has now launched his art and photography business. He is an artist who sells prints of the best and artwork in stock, great for home décor and affordability. This is his store when he sells his artwork: https://athulmvikas.wixsite.com/athulvwebsite/store-page

When it comes to photography, he has always been interested in capturing photos and videos from a young age though most of his projects have been through a simple iPhone 5s. As his enthusiasm evolved, he started using a Canon DSLR and it is ideal for his purpose.

All the memories are stored into a single shot and its value grows as time passes. That thought intrigues him to motivate him to capture high quality, memorable pictures that will tell its own story in the long run. He won a Silver Key for Photography via Scholastic Regional Writing and Art Competition in 2019 for Water frozen in time.

He focuses on depicting thoughts and emotions through the images while conducting photoshoots for clients, ranging from Portrait and event photography. He hopes to travel in the future and capture moments that tell unique stories.
